## Observations
- **Quality is deterministic** (cognitive % and OCR % at `±0%`) — the strongest regression signal for future checkpoints.
- **Gemma is stronger but heavier**: cognitive 54–62% vs Qwen 47–55%, at ~2–3× the encode/TTFT and much longer full-inference (iPhone CPU 34 s, s26 GPU 26 s).
- **Desktop GPU is dramatically faster** than CPU (Linux/Windows Qwen ~30 ms encode / ~135 ms full).
- **`†` cells** (Linux/Windows CPU) reflect one-run self-hosted-runner contention — the median is the representative value.
- **s26 OCR is lower** (~67–79% vs ~90% elsewhere), and **pixel9 GPU is slower than its CPU yet scores higher OCR** (Qwen GPU 82.2% vs CPU 67.0%) — device-backend traits worth tracking.
